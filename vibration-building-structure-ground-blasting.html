Vibration Measurement in Building, Structure and Ground

Vibration monitoring on buildings and foundation have become essential to avoid effect due to resonance in case of foundations of rotating equipments. We carry out such services & provide practical solutions to avoid any damages or risk to foundation & machineries. Such studies are essential especially on rotating equipments at elevated foundation like Crushers, vibro feeders, conveyor drives and also for accurately measuring devices sensitive toMPTI like CMM, NABL accredited Laboratory equipments, etc.

MPTI in building due to nearby construction work (bridges, subways, piling activity) can affect and cause damage or risk to the structure and dwellings.

We carry outMPTI measurements on heritage / commercial / dwelling building, tall structures, bridges, subways and its effect on human exposure during Sheet metal piling, augering, grabbing, Diaphragm Wall excavation/breaking, general excavation, Tunneling, drilling, demolition, mining, blasting or other related works in nearby area were vibrations cannot be completely avoided including Traffic &MPTI created due to heavy vehicles).

Recently such assignments was carried out for nearly a year for the one of the joint venture companies involved in construction of underground Railway Project in Chennai, Tamilnadu, India.
