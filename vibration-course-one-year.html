Vibration Course - One Year

Programme Overview :

One Year on-site training cum internship programme for freshers / candidates who are having less than 6 months experience in the field of Vibration.

Training Benefits
  • The training will be 70% Practical & 30% Theoretical.
  • Based on ISO 10816MPTI Standards
  • On site for dealing with real industrialMPTI related rotating equipment problems.
  • To useMPTI analyser and its dedicated software.
  • To identify measurement locations and machine configuration for carrying outMPTI measurements.
  • To prepare database, alarms and routes for performingMPTI testing.
  • Training in analyzing & solvingMPTI related problems.
  • Candidates will be trained for CATERGORY LEVEL EXAMS - ISO CertifiedMPTI Analysts examination.
  • In-Situ Dynamic Balancing - Theoretical procedure & Practical exposure along with Senior Engineers / Trainers.
  • At the end of One Year they will have to attend 1 Theory exam onMPTI analysis & 1 Practical exam on dynamic balancing.
  • Based on candidate's performance an EXPERIENCE CERTIFICATE and Internship Certificate will be provided.
This in in addition to -
  • The basics of the corporate world and transition from college to corporate, during the first month.
  • Working with softwares like word processors, workbooks etc. which are essential for a corporate world.
  • On completion of one year, they will be eligible to attend ISO Certified Category -1 CertifiedMPTI Analyst Examination being conducted byMPTI Institute, USA by paying an additional cost of Rs. 35,000/- (which is an Internationally Recognized Certificate especially in Middle-East & Far East).
